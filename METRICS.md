# Product Metrics (EQ AI)

## 1. North Star Metric
- **Total Identifiable Savings (TIS):** The dollar amount of annual savings surfaced for all users through the audit engine.

## 2. Secondary Metrics
- **Optimization Rate:** Percentage of audits that result in a "Plan Change" recommendation.
- **Credex Conversion:** Number of users who click "Explore Credits" after seeing their audit results.
- **Viral Coefficient:** Number of audit result URLs shared on social platforms.
